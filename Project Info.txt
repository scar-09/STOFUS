**Overview**
Stofus is a browser extension designed to help you stay productive by blocking distracting websites and content. It's a simple tool that allows you to set timers for specific sites you want to avoid, helping you maintain focus on your important tasks.

**Functionality**
- **URL Blocker**: Easily block any website by entering its URL and setting a focus duration from 1 to 3600 minutes.
- **Content Filter**: Block or blur specific keywords to hide distracting content across all websites.
- **Real-time Dashboard**: Track your active blocks, focus time, and daily streaks to stay motivated.
- **Quick Undo**: Quickly reverse your last block action if you made a mistake.
- **Customizable Settings**: Choose whether to completely block sites or just blur content based on keywords.

**Tech Stack**
- **Core Technologies**: HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript (ES6+).
- **Extension Framework**: Chrome Extension Manifest V3.
- **APIs**:
    - **Chrome Storage API**: For saving your blocked sites, keywords, and focus stats.
    - **Chrome Alarms API**: For managing focus timers and block expirations.
    - **Chrome Tabs & WebNavigation APIs**: For detecting and redirecting distracting websites.
- **Design & UI**: Uses the Montserrat font family and a modern, responsive layout with a dynamic background.
